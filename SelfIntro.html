As a Bachelor of Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo, not only I possess thorough theoretical knowledge in computer programming, I am also equipped with strong programming skills in Java, Scheme, C, C++, Shell, and Python as well as web development capabilities in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, and PHP. Through enrolling in numerous relevant courses such as CS 135: Designing Functional Programs, I was introduced to the fundamentals of computer science through the application of elementary programming patterns in the functional style of programming. I applied various programming methodologies in completing assignments including tracing via substitution, linear and nonlinear data structures, recursive data definitions, abstraction and encapsulation, and generative and structural recursion. Moreover, the CS 246: Object-Oriented Software Development course introduced me to object-oriented programming and the tools and techniques for software development such as selecting appropriate data structures and control structures, writing reusable codes, and reusing existing codes. The course also covers topics including designing, coding, debugging, testing, and documenting medium-sized programs.
